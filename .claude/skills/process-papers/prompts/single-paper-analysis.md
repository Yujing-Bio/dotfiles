# Single-Paper Deep Analysis Instructions

When only one paper is provided, replace the cross-paper synthesis with a deeper single-paper analysis.

## Structure

### Extended Summary
A thorough summary that goes beyond the per-paper summary — include nuances, secondary findings, and methodological details.

### Methodology Critique
Assess the methodology:
- Is the experimental design sound?
- Are there confounders or biases?
- Is the sample size adequate?
- Are the statistical methods appropriate?
- Could the results be explained by alternative hypotheses?

### Contribution Assessment
- What is genuinely novel about this work?
- How does it relate to the broader field? (use WebSearch to check recent related work)
- What does it enable that wasn't possible before?

### Open Questions
- What follow-up experiments or studies would strengthen or extend this work?
- What assumptions should be tested?
- What related problems does this open up?

## Style

Write as a thorough paper review — balanced, specific, and constructive.

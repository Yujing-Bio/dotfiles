# Detailed Synthesis Instructions

Write a multi-section literature review that connects and analyzes the papers in this collection.

## Structure

### Thematic Overview
What field or research question unites these papers? What is the current state of this area?

### Methodological Landscape
What approaches are being used? Are there dominant methodologies? How do they compare in rigor, scalability, or applicability?

### Key Findings and Consensus
Where do the papers agree? What results are replicated or mutually supported?

### Tensions and Contradictions
Where do the papers disagree? What conflicting evidence exists? What might explain the disagreements?

### Gaps and Open Questions
What isn't addressed by any of the papers? What questions remain unanswered? What would the next study need to investigate?

### Field Trajectory
Based on these papers, where is the field heading? What trends are emerging? What might change in the next 2-3 years?

## Style

Write in clear academic prose. Support claims by referencing specific papers (by title). This should read like a mini literature review that a researcher could use to quickly understand the state of the field.

# Brief Summary Instructions

Generate a concise summary of this paper in 3-5 bullet points.

## Format

**[Paper Title]**
- [Bullet 1: What is the paper's main objective/research question?]
- [Bullet 2: What is the key finding or result?]
- [Bullet 3: What is the main contribution to the field?]
- [Optional Bullet 4-5: Notable methodology or surprising result]

**Paper type:** [AI/ML | Non-AI/ML]

## AI/ML Classification

Flag the paper as **AI/ML** if it:
- Proposes or evaluates a machine learning model, neural network architecture, or training method
- Applies ML/AI as the primary methodology (not just using standard statistical methods)
- Focuses on topics like: deep learning, NLP, computer vision, reinforcement learning, generative models, foundation models, representation learning

Flag as **Non-AI/ML** otherwise — including papers that use basic statistical analysis, wet-lab biology, clinical studies without ML components, etc.

## Non-English Papers

If the paper is not in English, produce the summary in English. Do your best to accurately translate and summarize the content.

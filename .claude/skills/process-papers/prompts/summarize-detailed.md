# Detailed Summary Instructions

Generate a comprehensive structured summary of this paper.

## Format

**[Paper Title]**

- **Objective:** What problem does this paper address? What is the research question or hypothesis?

- **Methodology:** What approach, methods, or techniques were used? Include experimental design, datasets, models, or analytical frameworks.

- **Key Findings:** What are the main results? Include quantitative results where available (metrics, p-values, performance numbers).

- **Limitations:** What are the acknowledged or apparent limitations? What doesn't the paper address? What could weaken the conclusions?

- **Contributions:** What is novel about this work? How does it advance the field? What does it enable that wasn't possible before?

**Paper type:** [AI/ML | Non-AI/ML]

## AI/ML Classification

Flag the paper as **AI/ML** if it:
- Proposes or evaluates a machine learning model, neural network architecture, or training method
- Applies ML/AI as the primary methodology (not just using standard statistical methods)
- Focuses on topics like: deep learning, NLP, computer vision, reinforcement learning, generative models, foundation models, representation learning, spatial transcriptomics with ML

Flag as **Non-AI/ML** otherwise.

## Non-English Papers

If the paper is not in English, produce the summary in English. Do your best to accurately translate and summarize the content.
